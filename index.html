INTRODUCTION

Thanks to Love Story’s advanced neural network, we match users based on a comprehensive array of data points, ensuring maximum compatibility. Our matches are designed to lead to deeper conversations, meaningful connections, and, ultimately, successful long-term relationships.

To achieve the best results, please follow these instructions while completing the questionnaire:

  • All questions require an answer.
  • All questions should be answered honestly to ensure accurate matching.
  • To the extent possible, avoid selecting the “Decline to answer” or “Other” options.
  • You don’t need to select the maximum number of choices allowed in each question.

Rest assured, your responses won’t be lost if you accidentally go back, go forward, or refresh the page. However, do not close the window as that will result in the loss of your information.

At the end of the questionnaire, you can include your email address to receive a copy of your answers.
